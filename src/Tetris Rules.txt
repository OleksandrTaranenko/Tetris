At the beginning of the game the game speed is 3
When 1 line is completed, it disappears,
		you get 5 points and the speed of the game is increased by 1 
		
When 2 lines are completed, they disappear, 
		you get 12 points and the speed of the game is decreased by 1
		
When 3 lines are completed, they disappear, 
		you get 20 points and the speed of the game is decreased by 2
		
When 4 lines are completed, they disappear,  
		you get 30 points and the speed of the game is decreased by 3 

Keyboard control:
space - pause
A, Left - left
W, Up - rotate
D, Right - right
S, Down - down
